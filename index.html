Canine rattlesnake avoidance training

We specialize in K-9 rattlesnake avoidance training. We have trained thousands of dogs in the Tucson area to avoid rattlesnakes with a success rate of 100%! Colorado/Sonoran River Toad avoidance training is also available. Customer and veterinarian references are available upon request.

Our snake handler is Steve Reaves, an Arizona Department of Game and Fish licensed Wildlife Service Provider. Steve has been working with rattlesnakes in Arizona for over 18 years. None of his snakes have been de-fanged, de-venomized or mutilated in any way.

Steve's services include reptile education classes (free to schools and non-profit organizations) and snake-wrangling for films and documentaries. Steve is also a certified professional dog trainer and a member of the International Association of Canine Professionals and the National K-9 School for Dog Trainers. He is a graduate of National K-9 School for Dog Trainer Master Trainer Program in Columbus, Ohio.

We also train dogs in puppy development, basic and advanced obedience, behavior modification, scent detection, soft-mouth retrieval and tracking.
